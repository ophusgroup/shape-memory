# Results

## Composition and alloying

The elastocaloric response depends on composition. Substituting elements onto the Ni or Ti sublattice shifts the transformation stress, the latent heat, and the shape of the hysteresis loop. The widget below overlays each doped cell's response on the pure equiatomic NiTi reference.

:::{anywidget} ./widgets/compare.js
{
  "index_url": "../widgets/data/compare_index.json"
}
:::

Doped datasets are generated by the same loading protocol as the reference, with a few atoms substituted onto the Ni or Ti sublattice.

## Single-crystal reference

The equiatomic NiTi single crystal shows a clean stress-induced transformation: an elastic rise, a transformation event where the energy drops into the martensite well, and a heat-release spike. The closed cooling half of the cycle requires finite temperature, discussed in the [methods](methods.md).
